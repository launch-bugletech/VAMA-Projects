Our Story

A Gap in the Market
Became Our Purpose.

VAMA Projects was founded with one clear observation: the Indian market was delivering museums and experiential spaces in pieces. Design firms couldn't build. Civil contractors couldn't create experiences. AV companies couldn't handle the infrastructure. No one was accountable for the full outcome.

We built VAMA to close that gap an integrated company that brings every capability in-house, so that clients deal with one team, sign one contract, and receive one finished environment that works as intended from day one.

Over two decades, we've grown from a regional project executor to a multi-division national company with seven structured product lines and an active international pipeline without losing the on-ground accountability that defines how we work.

How We Operate

One Contract.
Total Accountability.

VAMA does not operate as separate departments that hand off to each other. Every division civil, electrical, AV, content, mechatronics works under a single project manager on a single contract. The client has one point of contact and one point of accountability throughout the project lifecycle.

This is what makes the difference between a space that opens beautifully and one that continues to perform for years.
